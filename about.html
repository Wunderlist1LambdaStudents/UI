What Exactly is Wunderlist 2.0?

Wunderlist is a cross-platform, easy to use application for keeping track of all you important lists and reminders in one place

Our Vision

At Wunderlist 2.0, we value your time. In order to help you stay as organized as possible and stay on track by maximizing your efficiency with time managment. Our goal is to provide you an easy to use application where you can easily keep track of everything you need to do. We asked ourselves what customers really need. Why write daily tasks everyday such as meditaing or excercising everyday when you can set it up as a daily reminder already included in your to do list.
